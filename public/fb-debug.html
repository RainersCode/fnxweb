Facebook Sharing Debugger

Use this tool to check how Facebook sees your website when sharing. It will show you the parsed Open Graph meta tags and let you refresh Facebook's cache.

Open Facebook Sharing Debugger

Common Issues

  • Image not showing: Make sure your OG image is at least 1200x630 pixels and less than 8MB.
  • Old content showing: Use the Sharing Debugger to scrape your URL again to update Facebook's cache.
  • Wrong description/title: Check your OG meta tags for errors.
